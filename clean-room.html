Finding

The firewall

Team A documents business requirements and workflows. Team B builds original software from that approved scope. The vendor's code and private implementation materials stay out.

Finding

Established practice, not a loophole

Clean-room reimplementation is presented as a known software practice, not as legal advice or a universal shield. Counsel review is still required.

Finding

What we never do

No source-code copying, no reverse-engineering language, no vendor private materials, no promise that every screen detail must be identical.
