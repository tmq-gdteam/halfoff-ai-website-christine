Finding

What the table shows

  • Every alternative except a custom replacement leaves the platform vendor- or vendor-of-record-owned.
  • Renegotiation and discounts relieve cost temporarily; a managed replacement changes the cost structure.
  • Agencies leave execution risk with the client; low-code can recreate the lock-in you're escaping.
  • A fixed managed replacement fits best when workflows are stable and spend is material.
