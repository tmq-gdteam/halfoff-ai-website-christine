Hard questions, plainly

FAQ

A skeptical buyer should not have to hunt for the hard questions. This page answers them directly and avoids unsupported proof.

01Is this copying our vendor's software?

No. The intended process is a clean-room reimplementation: requirements and workflows are documented, then original software is built from that scope. Vendor code, private materials, and copy-oriented work are out of bounds.

02How can a custom build cost less than enterprise SaaS?

The premise is narrower scope and multi-year amortization. You are not rebuilding a whole vendor suite. You are replacing the workflows your team relies on.

03Do you guarantee no downtime?

No absolute no-downtime claim is made here. The migration is designed as a phased process with sync, testing, cutover, and support so continuity is planned explicitly.

04What is included in the managed fee?

The site frames build, migration, hosting, security patching, and break-fix maintenance as included. Net-new features and expansion work belong in a separate SOW.

05Why request an assessment instead of publishing a price?

The price depends on current platform, spend, workflow scope, data complexity, and operating requirements. Publishing unsupported tiers would weaken trust.
