Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

halfoff.ai respects the privacy of the companies and people who visit our website, request a software savings assessment, or communicate with us about our services.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and the choices you have. It applies to information collected through the halfoff.ai website, lead forms, savings assessment requests, emails, and related pre-sales communications.

This Privacy Policy does not govern information handled under a signed customer agreement. If your company becomes a halfoff.ai customer, the processing of company data, migration data, platform data, credentials, workflow documents, or other implementation materials will be governed by a separate written agreement, data processing terms, security terms, and any applicable statement of work.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you choose to provide and limited technical information generated when you use the website.

Information you provide directly

When you contact us or request a savings assessment, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Business email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Job title or role
  • Company website
  • Current software platform or vendor
  • Estimated software spend
  • Contract renewal timing
  • Number of users or seats
  • Workflow or use-case details you choose to submit
  • Messages, notes, files, or other information you send to us

You should not submit confidential customer data, regulated data, production exports, passwords, API keys, health information, payment card numbers, employee records, or other sensitive information through a public website form. If a deeper review requires sensitive information, we will provide a controlled process under a separate agreement.

Information collected automatically

When you visit the website, we may collect limited technical and usage information, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Referring page
  • Pages viewed
  • Time and date of visit
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Interactions with forms, calculators, buttons, and pages

This information helps us understand how the website performs, improve the user experience, protect the site from misuse, and measure interest in our services.

Cookies and similar technologies

We may use cookies, pixels, analytics tools, and similar technologies to operate the website, remember preferences, understand traffic, improve content, and measure marketing performance.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect some website features, including calculators, saved preferences, or analytics-based improvements.

2. How we use information

We use information for business and website purposes, including to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Prepare or discuss a software savings assessment
  • Evaluate whether halfoff.ai may be a fit for your company
  • Understand your current software environment at a high level
  • Communicate about our services
  • Schedule meetings or follow-ups
  • Improve the website, messaging, calculators, and user experience
  • Maintain website security and prevent abuse
  • Analyze traffic and campaign performance
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights, users, customers, and business

We do not use website form submissions to make automated legal, employment, credit, insurance, or similarly significant decisions about individuals.

3. How we share information

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate the website and business, such as:

  • Website hosting providers
  • Form and CRM tools
  • Email and communication providers
  • Scheduling tools
  • Analytics providers
  • Security and fraud-prevention providers
  • Professional advisors, including attorneys, accountants, and consultants

These providers may access information only as needed to provide services to us and are expected to protect it appropriately.

We may also share information:

  • If required by law, subpoena, court order, or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of halfoff.ai, our users, customers, or others
  • In connection with a business transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, or sale of assets
  • With your consent or at your direction

4. Customer and assessment data

A public website assessment request is only an initial business inquiry. It is not a production migration, technical audit, or contractual engagement.

If your company later enters into a paid engagement with halfoff.ai, any software access, data exports, workflow documentation, credentials, source materials, integration details, or migration-related information will be handled under separate written terms. Those terms should define scope, confidentiality, data processing, security, access controls, retention, deletion, and responsibility for customer-provided materials.

5. Data retention

We keep personal information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including to respond to your inquiry, manage business records, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Typical pre-sales inquiry and assessment information may be retained while we are actively communicating with your company and for a reasonable period afterward for business, audit, and legal purposes.

When information is no longer needed, we delete, de-identify, or archive it according to our internal retention practices.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

No website, email system, internet transmission, or storage system is completely secure. For that reason, do not send highly sensitive information through public website forms or ordinary email unless we have provided a secure process for that purpose.

7. Your choices

You may contact us to request that we:

  • Confirm whether we hold information about you
  • Correct inaccurate contact information
  • Delete information you provided, where legally and operationally possible
  • Stop sending marketing communications
  • Limit certain uses of your information, where applicable

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in the email or by contacting us at privacy@halfoff.ai.

We may need to verify your identity or authority before completing certain requests.

8. California privacy notice

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under California privacy laws depending on how those laws apply to halfoff.ai and the nature of your interaction with us.

Subject to applicable requirements and exceptions, California residents may have the right to request:

  • The categories of personal information collected
  • The categories of sources of personal information
  • The business or commercial purposes for collecting personal information
  • The categories of third parties to whom information is disclosed
  • Access to certain personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion of certain personal information
  • Opt-out of certain selling or sharing of personal information, if applicable
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

halfoff.ai does not sell personal information. If our practices change in a way that requires a “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information” mechanism, we will update this Privacy Policy and provide the required choice mechanism.

To make a California privacy request, contact privacy@halfoff.ai.

9. International visitors

halfoff.ai is intended for business users and companies that are evaluating our services. If you access the website from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

If we engage with a company in a jurisdiction that requires additional privacy or data processing terms, those terms should be addressed in the applicable written agreement before any implementation, migration, or production data review begins.

10. Children’s privacy

The website and services are intended for businesses and are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact privacy@halfoff.ai and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

11. Third-party links

The website may link to third-party websites, tools, or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, security, or content of those third parties. Review their policies before providing information to them.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the website after the updated Privacy Policy is posted means the updated policy applies to your future use of the website.
