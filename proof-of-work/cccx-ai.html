Engineering approach and capability

The problem

The challenge

Contact-center operations are a textbook case of SaaS bloat: separate, compounding per-seat licenses for telephony, sentiment/QA, compliance redaction, and agent coaching  tools that rarely integrate cleanly, leaving fractured data and a poor operator experience.

The approach

How it was engineered

cccx.ai consolidates voice, chat, email, and social into a single interface, layered with real-time AI tooling and automated redaction of sensitive data, all inside one secure environment  without bolting third-party middleware on top.
